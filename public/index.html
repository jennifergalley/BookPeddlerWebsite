Prices

Our books all come from local trade-ins, so we are able to give you the best price in town! That's the power of shopping locally!

Credit Policy

We give 15% of the publisher’s cover price in credit for paperbacks. Hardcover credit varies depending on the salability of the book. We are sometimes limited in shelf and storage space, so we may not be able to use everything you bring in, every time.

When you purchase books you may use your credit to pay for 30% of your total sale. Remaining credit is kept on file for future visits.

We do not accept Reader’s Digest Condensed books or encyclopedias. Please do not bring in books that are torn, water damaged or stained; as we will not be able to use them.

Unless other arrangements are made any books we cannot use will be donated to the Salvation Army or Disabled Veterans thrift stores.
